For the fluid scaling between breakpoints (the clamp functions), rem is often better because:

Precision: High-fidelity designs often use "off-grid" numbers to fit a specific aspect ratio (like 1.1875rem for 19px).
Math: clamp() requires a vw or % unit in the middle, which theme.spacing() doesn't provide.
Accessibility: rem units automatically resize if the user changes their browser's "Default Font Size," whereas spacing() pixels might remain static depending on your theme setup.
